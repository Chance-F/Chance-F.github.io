About

League of Legends is one of the number one played online games world wide. League of Legends AKA League, is what is known as a MOBA. MOBA stands for Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. League is developed by Riot Games for Windows and Mac OS. In game the player has the option to play 148 characters called "Champions". Each Champion has its own unique set of abilities as well as their own unique play style ranging from high range marskman to close range melee champions. Many champions fall into a specific category such as "Assassin, Tank, Marksman, Support, Mage, and Fighter". Below I have inlcuded some examples of each!
